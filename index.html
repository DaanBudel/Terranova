Terra do Cafe

Burlap‑Born Coffee • Serra da Mantiqueira

From Burlap to Brew

Every Terra do Cafe bean rests for 30 days in jute sacks—breathing the cool mountain air—before hitting our zero‑emission roaster in Belo Horizonte. The result: velvet sweetness and terroir clarity you can’t achieve in steel silos.

We sign each sack with the farmer’s name, harvest date, and altitude, because provenance should be as visible as crema.
